Our Story

Redefining Premium Travel

Founded in 2018, DriveElite was built with a singular mission: to simplify the process of luxury vehicle rentals by eliminating hidden charges, complex contracts, and lengthy verification lines.

We believe renting a premium vehicle should be as exhilarating as driving one. By integrating modern digital check-ins, pre-rental camera verification, and responsive customer helpdesks, we ensure a seamless and transparent transaction from pickup to return.

Our Journey

Milestones of Growth

2018

Company Founded

DriveElite opened its first showroom in Manhattan, NY with a curated fleet of 15 premium luxury sedans.

2020

Fleet & City Expansion

Expanded operations to Miami and Los Angeles, growing our garage sizes to 80+ supercars and premium SUVs.

2022

Technology Integration

Launched the digital member portal including dynamic status tracking, pre-rental inspections, and instant duration extensions.

2024

Green Mobility Pledge

Added high-end luxury electric vehicles (Tesla, Porsche Taycan, Audi e-tron) to support sustainable luxury travel.

2026

Enterprise Client Services

Introduced corporate business fleet management, reaching over 50,000 satisfied members nationwide.
