Pre-Rental Vehicle Verification FAQ

Verification captures physical status before handover. Pre-rental inspection reports protect you, ensuring no liability charges apply for pre-existing scratches.

The camera upload button unlocks on your dashboard active rentals screen once pickup starts, or after physical keys are checked out by showroom dispatchers.

We request 4 exterior corner images, 1 interior dashboard console image, and 1 fuel/charge gauge reading image to generate verified reports.

Uploaded pictures are saved in the client contract files. Verification is marked completed, and key handover is approved immediately.

Rental Duration Extensions

Go to Dashboard > Extend Duration. Specify extra days, review billing breakdowns (daily rate remains same), and submit requests to admin panels.

Submit requests at least 12 hours before original return deadlines. Late requests risk conflicts with subsequent reservations, leading to decline outcomes.

Our team verifies next booking reserves. If clear, the extension is approved, new timelines are set, and deposit authorizations remain unchanged.

Approval notifications alerts are dispatched via email and show up instantly under notifications in your dashboard. Active rental returns dates auto-adjust.

Profile & Account Help

Click Register, enter credentials, verify email addresses, upload your NY driver license file, and wait 15 minutes for security status validation.

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login screen, enter your registered email address, click the recovery link sent to your inbox (expires in 15 mins), and specify new strong passwords.

Log into Profile, select Personal Details or Address, update information, upload new documents if license expires, and click save options.

Under Profile Settings, use the toggle switches to choose whether to receive email notices, browser push alerts, or promotional seasonal discount updates.

Notifications & Alerts FAQ

Reminders protect drivers from late return penalties. Alerts are automatically triggered 24 hours and 6 hours before contract expirations.

Status changes (Review → Approved/Rejected) automatically trigger instant alerts in your inbox and send copy PDFs to your email.

Scheduled pickups and returns details are pushed to notifications to guide you on showroom addresses and grace periods.

Billing & Security Deposits

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept direct bank transfers for long-term rentals. Cash and prepaid cards are not accepted.

Yes, a security deposit is required for all vehicles. The amount ranges from $1,000 to $2,500 depending on the vehicle class. This is processed as a temporary authorization hold on your credit card.

The security deposit authorization hold is released immediately upon safe return of the vehicle with no new damage. It typically takes 3 to 5 business days to clear, depending on your card issuer.

General Policies & Guidelines

Drivers must be 21+ years (25+ for high performance sports supercars), hold a physical original driving license valid in NY, and submit security deposits.

Free cancellation up to 24h prior to pickup. Cancellations within 24h are charged a flat rate penalty of 1 day's rental fee, with rest refunded.

We offer a 59-minute grace period. Later returns trigger hourly penalty rates automatically unless extensions were previously approved.

Comprehensive coverage is included. Drivers are liable for insurance deductibles ($500) if damage reports occur during active rental periods.
