Privacy Policy

Learn how Drive Elite collects, uses, protects, and manages your personal information.

  3. Privacy Policy
Last Updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DriveElite ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal info, please contact us.

"Your privacy is our core guarantee. We design every booking tracker, verification camera tool, and extension log with data safety protocols."

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the portal, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our fleet range, or when you contact customer support desks.

Key Categories Collected:

  • •
    Personal Identifiers: First and last name, date of birth, gender, and digital copies of driving licenses.
  • •
    Booking Details: Vehicles rented, showroom pickup/return locations, rental duration periods, and invoice amounts.
  • •
    Rental Diagnostics: Camera verification condition photos uploaded at pickup or return.
  • •
    Communications: Email logs, advisor live chat recordings, and option 9 helpline transcripts.
  • •
    Device Metrics: Trusted login devices, IP locations, browser user agents.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract agreements with you, compliance with legal obligations, and/or your consent.

Specifically, personal drivers data is analyzed to verify license validities, coordinate showroom dispatchers, update active trackers, notify of late return alerts, and evaluate star rating feedback reviews.

4. Information Sharing

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business contract obligations. We do not sell driver details databases to third-party marketing directories.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Specific details about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies are set out in our Cookie Notice.

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. Driver license photos and payment histories are encrypted using standard AES-256 protocols.

7. User Rights

In some regions (like the EEA and UK), you have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. These may include the right to request access and obtain a copy of your personal info, request rectification or erasure, or restrict processing.

You can review or update details under account Profile Settings, or toggle notification choices (Email, Browser reminders, Newsletters) at any time.

8. Third-Party Services

We may link to or incorporate third-party services (like payment gateways, insurance providers, and mapping platforms). We are not responsible for their independent data handling policies, and suggest reviewing their guidelines.

9. Data Retention

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (such as tax or audit requirements).

10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under 18 years of age. By using the services, you represent that you are at least 18.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
