5. Booking & Agreement Terms

Submitting requests queues the vehicle but does not finalize contracts. Rental durations are strictly defined on the booking preview. Exposing vehicles to unapproved drivers violates agreement clauses, leading to instant cancellation and deposit forfeitures.

6. Vehicle Usage Rules

DriveElite vehicles are designed for luxury personal transit. Prohibited activities include:

  • Towing other vehicles or trailering.
  • Racing, track-day sprints, or commercial transportations (ridesharing/delivery).
  • Driving under influence of alcohol or narcotic substances.
  • Transporting pets or smoking inside cabins (fees apply for interior detailing).
