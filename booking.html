Policies

Rental Terms & Conditions

All rentals require a valid driver's license and security deposit. Vehicles must be returned in the same condition as received. Mileage limits apply — check your specific vehicle terms for details.

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 25% charge. No-shows are charged the full rental amount.

Renters are responsible for any damage during the rental period. Vehicle condition is documented through our 6-point photo inspection system. Basic CDW insurance is included.

Basic Collision Damage Waiver is included. Premium zero-deductible coverage is available at an additional cost. Third-party insurance is accepted if it meets minimum requirements.

Vehicles are provided with a full tank. Return with full tank to avoid refueling charges of $5/gallon plus a $25 service fee.

Late returns incur $50/hr surcharge. Returns more than 4 hours late are charged an additional full day. Contact us to extend and avoid surcharges.

Extensions can be requested up to 2 hours before the scheduled return time. Subject to availability and admin approval. Extended rates match original booking terms.

Vehicle Inspection
