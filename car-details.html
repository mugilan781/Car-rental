Overview

Vehicle Description

The Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S 4MATIC+ is the pinnacle of performance luxury. Powered by a handcrafted 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine producing 630 horsepower, it accelerates from 0-60 mph in just 3.1 seconds. The AMG-specific 9-speed automatic transmission delivers power seamlessly through the sophisticated 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system.

Inside, you'll find a cabin that rivals the finest luxury sedans. Nappa leather upholstery, ambient lighting with 64 colors, and the Burmester® High-End 3D Surround Sound System create an atmosphere of supreme refinement. The MBUX infotainment system with dual 12.3-inch displays puts every function at your fingertips.

Safety is paramount with features including Pre-Safe®, Active Brake Assist, Blind Spot Assist, and 9 airbags. Whether you're navigating city streets or conquering open highways, the AMG GT 63 S delivers an unforgettable driving experience that perfectly balances raw power with refined luxury.

Policies

Rental Terms & Conditions

Please review the following policies before booking.

Bookings must be made at least 2 hours before the desired pickup time. A valid credit card is required at the time of booking. The full rental amount and security deposit will be charged upon approval. Same-day bookings are subject to vehicle availability.

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 25% charge. No-shows will be charged the full rental amount. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Vehicles are provided with a full tank. Renters must return the vehicle with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel, a refueling charge of $5/gallon plus a $25 service fee will apply.

Any damage to the vehicle during the rental period is the responsibility of the renter. Vehicle condition is documented before and after each rental through our 6-point photo verification system. Basic insurance coverage is included; additional coverage options are available.

Late returns incur a surcharge of $50 per additional hour. Returns more than 4 hours late will be charged for an additional full day. Please contact us if you need to extend your rental to avoid surcharges.

Rental extensions can be requested through your dashboard up to 2 hours before the scheduled return time. Extensions are subject to vehicle availability and admin approval. Extended rental rates apply based on the original booking terms.

Basic CDW (Collision Damage Waiver) is included with every rental. Premium insurance packages with zero-deductible coverage are available. Third-party insurance documentation is accepted if it meets our minimum coverage requirements.
